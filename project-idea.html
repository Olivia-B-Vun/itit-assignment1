The Problem

Hint: It's Me!

If you know me (which I will assume you don’t) I really like organisation, and everything fits into a neat little box. To facilitate this, I use a range of online and offline tools. Some of the tools I currently use include:

  • Dog Board
  • Google Calendar & Google Sheets
  • House Board
  • My Fitness Pal
  • Stronglifts
  • Trello
  • Whiteboard

As you can imagine, it’s a lot of manual work to keep the information up to date and based on the functionality descriptions (of the above tools) alone, there are a lot of redundancies. However, the one issue that I (and I’m sure many others like me face) is that once the app is closed, or I’ve turned away from the board; it ceases to function. Object permanence doesn’t exist and it’s not a concept that my brain seems willing to understand.
